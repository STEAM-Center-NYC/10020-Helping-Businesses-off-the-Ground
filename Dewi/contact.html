The majority of businesses planned seek funding through the Coronavirus Aid, Relief and Economic Security (CARES) Act however, many anticipated problems with accessing the program such as bureaucratic hassles and difficulties establishing eligibility. But our website will not only help struggling businesses, it will help create a new sense of community that was lost during the pandemic.

Our Mission

Our objective is to be of service to small businesses who have struggled due to the pandemic. This website builds a foundation to assist small businesses because they had widely varying beliefs about the likely duration of COVID-related disruptions. Even as we move out of the pandemic, businesses are financially fragile and find it burdensome in growing their customer base and bringing their products to new communities.

Our Vision

In the future we hope to at least collaborate with hundreds of smaller businesses to give them more customers and a platform. We want small businesses to have a better recovery from the pandemic, and to share their creations and services with more people.

About Us

We recently became extremely fascinated by the idea of helping one’s business, so the five of us came together to develop a website called “Get Together.” This website goal is to serve as a resource center for failed and in need businesses that people may not be aware about.
